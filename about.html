About Us

From Base Oils to Finished Fluids, Serving the World

Cassidy Energy delivers reliable, high-performance lubrication solutions for businesses of all sizes across industrial and automotive sectors. From premium base oils to fully formulated finished lubricants, we connect global manufacturers, blenders, and end-users with the products and expertise they need to operate efficiently and sustainably.

With strategic supply hubs and distribution capabilities spanning North America, Europe, Asia, and the Middle East, we ensure timely access to critical lubrication products — whether you're sourcing Group I–III base oils, advanced additive packages, or OEM-approved finished fluids. Our commitment to quality, technical support, and simplified logistics makes us a trusted partner in powering industry and protecting equipment worldwide.
